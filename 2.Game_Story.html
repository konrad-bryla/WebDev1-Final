The Story of Brylithia

Long ago, the Gods of Brylithia banded together in order to create a champion of the cosmos and as a result they gave life to the land of Brylithia in its own pocket of time and space as a training ground. The gods have created the Trials of Bryla which the inhabitants of Brylithia would have to uncover and take part in to become champions. Although, overjoyed and anxious for the first champion to be crowned. For thousands of years attempt after attempt, the very existence of the trials began to be whisked away by every breeze. The ancient ruins of civilizations buried under rock, forest, and disguised by nature not a soul could find even a spec of the trials.
